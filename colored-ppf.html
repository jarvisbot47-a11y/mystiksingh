Colored Paint Protection Film

Colored Paint Protection Film is the most advanced way to change your vehicle's appearance while preserving its paint. This film is significantly thicker than vinyl and offers self-healing technology, impact resistance, and UV protection.

Our installs prioritize absolute cleanliness, perfect alignment, and controlled tension so the film settles correctly and lasts for years. Every job includes a 2-week post-install inspection to ensure flawless adhesion.
